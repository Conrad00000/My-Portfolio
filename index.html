Bio

I have had a love for computers ever since I was in middle school. Where I learned that I could get out of my standardized testing by tricking the teachers into making them think my online account was broken. My whole young adult life has revolved around and relied upon computers. My first job was being hired as a computer repair technician for a local computer repair store in my sophomore year of high school. It is my exposure to computers that led me to pursue my computer science degree. I have now been actively learning to code for the past 4 years. I have now contributed to a few open-source projects on GitHub and helped develop a game with ISUs game club. I am a passionate developer who is more than willing to put in the extra work to develop your next project.
